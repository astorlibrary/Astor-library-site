How certainty is manufactured

The play’s predictions matter because characters interpret them, act upon them and mistake riddling language for safety.

The witches

A greeting is not an instruction

The sisters name possible futures, but Macbeth turns possibility into a private demand. The space between prophecy and action is where responsibility remains.

After Duncan

Power cannot finish the crime

The crown does not end Macbeth’s fear. Blood, sleep and the imagined return of the dead show that possession of power creates new dangers rather than closing the old ones.

Equivocation

Words become traps

The later apparitions tell truths in forms designed to mislead. Macbeth hears guarantees because he has learned to choose the meaning that best protects his confidence.

A useful way in. For each prophecy, write down its exact words, Macbeth’s interpretation and what actually happens. The differences reveal how the tragedy uses language.
