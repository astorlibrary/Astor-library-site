Hamlet: Sources, Texts and Contexts.

A guide to the early printed texts of Hamlet, the play’s source tradition, performance history and the political, religious and philosophical pressures around the tragedy.

Use this for: textual history, AO3 context and performance, especially the differences between the play’s three early printed texts.
