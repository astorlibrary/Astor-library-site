Where the argument sharpens

Gender in the novel is not one rule repeated from beginning to end. It shifts with work, desire, illness, knowledge and violence.

Mina

Skill praised and contained

Mina works, types, organises documents and repeatedly sees what the men miss. Their praise of her ‘man’s brain’ recognises ability while revealing how difficult they find it to imagine that ability as simply a woman’s.

Lucy

Desire becomes a public judgement

Lucy’s letters, three proposals, sleepwalking and transformation are interpreted by other people. Her story asks who is permitted appetite and what the novel imagines must happen when feminine desire becomes visible.

The men

Masculinity is performed too

The Crew of Light build fellowship through medicine, secrecy, grief and organised violence. Their confidence is often real, but so are their fear and dependence upon Mina’s intellectual labour.

A useful way in. Treat ‘Victorian gender roles’ as an argument, not a single rule. Ask which character is speaking, what the scene requires and whether the ending settles the tension it raises.
