Strange Case of Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde, Robert Louis Stevenson

Stevenson’s novella follows Gabriel Utterson as he investigates the connection between Dr Henry Jekyll and Edward Hyde. The case unfolds through wills, letters, witness statements and delayed disclosure.
