1726

Gulliver's Travels, Jonathan Swift

First published anonymously by Benjamin Motte in 1726, Swift’s four voyages move through Lilliput, Brobdingnag, Laputa and Houyhnhnmland. The four-part voyage parodies travel writing and satirises politics, science, colonialism, pride and human reason.

1740

Pamela; or, Virtue Rewarded, Samuel Richardson

Richardson’s anonymously published novel uses Pamela’s letters to examine servant–master power, virtue, consent and class. Its immediate controversies are traced through Shamela, Anti-Pamela, Highmore’s paintings and later responses.
