What matters here

The book always comes first.

History earns its place when it changes what we notice in the writing. A first edition, a disputed ending or a daring production can send us back to a familiar sentence with different eyes.

That is what Astor Library is for: careful reading, honest explanation and pages worth returning to. Nothing is finished for good. As the library grows, older pages will be checked, deepened and connected to the new ones.
