The idea behind the name

A reference library inside the book.

The original Astor Library in New York was not a circulating library. Its books were there to be consulted and studied. Astor Editions take that reference-library idea into the text itself.

Read straight through, or stop for a summary, a factual note or a piece of context. The help is there without taking the book away from you.
